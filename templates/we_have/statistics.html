The majority of students at the Samarkand State Institute of Foreign Languages

The majority of students at the Samarkand State Institute of Foreign Languages are female and under the age of 30. Most are enrolled in full-time programs. The student body primarily consists of citizens of the Republic of Uzbekistan, with a small number of international and stateless students.
